    • Grinding Methods
    The Way Your Coffee is Ground Effects the Flavor

    • Espresso machines work best with finely ground beans
    • A French press works best with a coarse ground roast of your choice
    • The pour over method works best with a medium grind
    • Metal and paper filters work best when the shape of the filter (cone or flat bottom) is taken into consideration
